The Bad Bat Escape

This was a game I made with a team at Global Game Jam 2017. The team consisted of people I met at the jam - it was my first time collaborating with strangers, but they were all lovely and talented people. Besides this, it was also my first ever game jam and my second ever game. All those things make this game quite special to me.

Game and Rules

The theme of the game jam was "Waves" and as such we thought of a game where you could only see by emitting sound waves like the echolocation a bat uses. That idea eventually evolved into a bat flying through a cave trying to outrun a horrible monster. At some interval the bat will fire out a blast of sound that will "illuminate" the surroundings for a short while. The player will then have to guide the bat through the cave while avoiding any rocks that might be in the way. If the bat hits three rocks, it will get eaten, and the game will end. Don't be discouraged if this happens a lot; the game is very hard.

Controls

To control the bat, you must swipe the screen (with mouse or finger) in the direction you want the bat to go. The bat is very slow though, so multiple swipes are needed to get it to move fast. You cannot control when the bat emits echolocation; it happens at preset times.

Play the game

By pressing one of the buttons below you can either play the game in you browser or download the game for you computer or Android device.

If you're having trouble playing the game in you browser, try another browser (Chrome doesn't work) or simply download the game.
