I swear; it's not blood from the eyes

I made this game as en entry for the Ludum Dare 40 Compo. The theme was: "The more you have the worse it is". It was my first Ludum Dare, the first game jam where I worked alone and also the first game jam that i co-organized. This might explain the weird title...

Game and Rules

The game is a castlevania-ish game with only one level. You jump around as an animated square and kills other squares to gain EXP. But there is a twist: the more EXP you have the worse it is... You can figure out what that means by yourself.

The Jam

This was only my second ever game jam, but either way I was part of organizing it. Normally Ludum Dare is done from home, but me and two others decided to host an in-person version of the jam at KADK in Copenhagen. It was a great experience and definately something I'll do again. Controls

You need an Xbox360 controller for each person playing this.

A to jump, X to slash your sword. You can hold down the jump-button to jump higher and also wall jump.
