The Abyss

This prototype was made for a university course in a single week by me and a team using Unity. The assignment was to make a game that was spectatable, that is, interesting to watch as well as play. I worked on the team as a gameplay-designer and programmer.

Game and Rules

We ended up making this game: The Abyss. It can be summed up as a local-multiplayer fighting game platformer with no hand to hand combat; instead, you defeat your opponent by outlasting, outsmarting or out-platforming them. When the game starts platforms with different interesting random properties will spawn and it is now your job to be the last man standing. If you touch the sides of the arena you're dead. If you touch the bottom of the screen you're dead. If you touch the dangerous red platforms you're also dead. There are many different kinds of platforms that work both with and against you, they are all activated after a small delay when you touch them, but I wont spoil the (somewhat frustrating!) experience of figuring out what platform does what.

Controls

Player 1: W to Jump, AD to move left and right

Player 2: Up-Arrow to jump, Left/Right-Arrow to move left and right

Both players can jump higher by holding down the jump-button for longer, as well as perform wall-jumps and wall-slides.

Play the game

To your right, you can either download the game or play it directly in your browser (Except for Chrome, use a different browser!)
