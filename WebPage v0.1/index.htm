My name is Mads Engberg and I am a Danish programmer/game-designer. My formal education is a BSc in software development and currently I am taking an MSc in games at ITU. I am a programmer first and a designer second. I love action games that aren't shooters, such as platformers or fighting games. I'm also a big fan of game-feel and creating character controllers that you want to master.
