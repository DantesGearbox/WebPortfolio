Who I am Game programmer and designer

All 'bout me!

You're currently looking at the "about-me" section, the place where I get to talk about myself a lot.

My name is Mads Engberg and I am a Danish programmer/game-designer. My formal education is a BSc in software development and currently I am taking an MSc in games at ITU in Denmark.

I am a programmer first and a designer second, but I espcially adore the part of game development that lies in the limbo between those two. The place where technology and design must work together to create gameplay with the correct game-feel and user experience. I love action games, such as platformers and fighting games and I'm a big fan of and creating games that you want to master.
