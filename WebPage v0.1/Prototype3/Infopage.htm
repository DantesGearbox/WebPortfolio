Pacman After Dark

This prototype was made for a university course in two weeks by me and a team using Unity. The assignment was to remake an old classic game with one or very few things changed. I worked on the team as a gameplay-designer and programmer.

Game and Rules

Given these guidelines we made this game: Pacman after dark. The inital idea was to make a stealth Pacman game. To deliver on this idea we changed the amount of Pacman-pellets to four; one in each corner of the map, you win by collecting all four. We also changed the AI of the ghosts to be more territorial, they circle around their respective pellets, protecting them. Once they spot you they start chasing after you and you have to move fast to get away with your life intact. We also removed the ability for Pacman to destroy the ghosts to keep the game focussed on sneaking.

What I think of this game

I actually think the idea of a stealth Pacman can if expanded upon. If there were more levels, maybe even randomly generated levels, and the Ghosts field of view got toned slightly down, this could be a fun little game. However, I don't see many ways to expand upon the stealth-theme without ruining the Pacman-esque-ness from it.

Controls

Arrow keys to move.

We tried mimicing the original Pacman controls, which means that if you input a direction while it is blocked, Pacman will go that direction whenever it becomes possible.
