Minimum Wage

This prototype was made for a university course in a single week by me and a team using Unity. The assignment was to use the game design concept of 'loops' to create any game. I worked on the team as a gameplay-designer and programmer.

Game and Rules

In Minimum Wage you play as Pierre, a frenchman with a wonderous moustache, but a very low paying job. Pierre sorts boxes in a warehouse. But Pierre has no time to hurry! His bosses only pay for every correctly delivered a box and they subtract from his payroll everytime he messes up; and as if that wasn't enough they also penalize him if he doesn't get all crates out of the trucks before they leave. Gotta go fast Pierre!

Controls

Arrow keys to move and space to pick up and let go of a crate.

Thankfully Pierre has very strong arms and can throw the crates. You do this by holding down the space bar while carrying a crate. The longer you hold it, the longer Pierre throws it.

Play the game

To your right, you can either download the game or play it directly in your browser (Except for Chrome, use a different browser!)
