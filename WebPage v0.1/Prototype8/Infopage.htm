Pizza Panic!

This prototype was made for a university course in a three weeks by and a team using Unity. The assignment was to make a game where there had to be an exchange of resources and only 1 looser.

Game and Rules

Pizza panic is a local multiplayer racing game, similar to Crazy Taxi, but instead of delivering people, you're delivering pizza. Your fuel levels go down all the time, but whenever you pick up a pizza, you magically get more. First person to run out of gas looses! Up to four players can drive around and even steal each others pizzas, by driving into each other and knocking the pizzas off of them.

Controls

You need an Xbox360 controller for each person playing this.

Accelerate - Right Trigger

Reverse - Left Trigger

Brake - A

Boost/Drive faster - X

Unstuck - Y

HONK HONK - B

Play the game

To your right, you can either download the game or play it directly in your browser (Except for Chrome, use a different browser!)
