Neon and Slow-Motion

This prototype was made for a university course in a single week by me alone using Unity. The assignment was to make a one-button-game.

Game and Rules

Your goal is to navigate the ball to the top of the level as fast as possible. You drag and let go of the ball to fire it in the direction you're dragging. While you're dragging time slows down by a factor 20, which should make it possible to navigate the mace in the top speed at all times. Currently there is only one level that can be reset indefinately.

Controls

Drag-n-let-go to propel the ball in a chosen direction. While dragging, the game moves in slow-mo.

Play the game

To your right, you can either download the game or play it directly in your browser (Except for Chrome, use a different browser!)

Keep in mind: though this game will work with a mouse, this game was meant for a touch-device.
